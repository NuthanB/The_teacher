DSSSB Exam Pattern for Non-Teaching Posts

The examination scheme for DSSSB Non Teaching posts- Lower Division Clerk, Junior Assistant, Stenographer Grade 2, Junior Stenographer (Hindi/English), Lower Division Clerk-cum-typist, Stenographer & Assistant Grade 1 has been discussed below. 

  1. As per the DSSSB Exam Pattern, there will be five subjects.
  2. There will be 200 multiple-choice questions (MCQs) carrying 01 mark each.
  3. The candidates will get 2 hours to appear for the test.
  4. There will be 0.25 negative markings for incorrect answers.
  5. Minimum Qualifying Marks for General/EWS is 40%, for OBC (Delhi) is 35% and for SC/ST/PH (PwD) is 30%.

DSSSB Exam Pattern for Section Officer (Horticulture)

There would be Tier 1 and Tier 2 Exams for Section Officer (Horticulture) posts. In Two Tier Examinations, the Tier-I examination is only qualifying in nature and used for short listing only. Further selection will be made based on cumulative merit/marks obtained in the Tier-II examination.The exam scheme for both tiers has been discussed here. 

  1. One mark shall be awarded to every correct answer.
  2. Questions are objective multiple-choice type questions for 300 Marks.
  3. There will be 2 Sections.
  4. The time duration of the Tier 1 exam is 2 hours.
  5. Negative marking of 0.25 marks will be deducted.
