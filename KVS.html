KVS Teaching Exam Information

Overview

The Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) conducts recruitment exams for various teaching positions in Kendriya Vidyalayas, central government schools across India. Below is detailed information about the exam:

Eligibility Criteria

Position Minimum Educational Qualification Age Limit
Primary Teacher (PRT) Senior Secondary with 50% marks and Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.)
OR
Senior Secondary with 50% marks and Diploma in Education (D.Ed.)
OR
Senior Secondary with 50% marks and Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.El.Ed.)		 Maximum 30 years
Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT) Bachelor's Degree with at least 50% marks in the concerned subject and in aggregate with B.Ed. or equivalent Maximum 35 years
Post Graduate Teacher (PGT) Master's Degree with at least 50% marks in the concerned subject and in aggregate with B.Ed. or equivalent Maximum 40 years

Application Process

To apply for the KVS teaching exam, candidates need to follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official website of KVS.
  2. Register for the exam by providing necessary details.
  3. Fill in the online application form with accurate information.
  4. Upload scanned copies of required documents such as educational certificates, photograph, and signature.
  5. Pay the application fee online through the provided payment gateway.
  6. Submit the application form and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.

Exam Pattern

The exam pattern for the KVS teaching exam typically includes the following sections:

  • Child Development and Pedagogy
  • Subject-specific topics
  • General Awareness and Current Affairs
  • Reasoning Ability
  • Numerical Ability
  • Language Proficiency (English and Hindi)

The exam may be conducted in online or offline mode and consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs). The number of questions and marks distribution vary based on the position and level of the exam.

Additional Details

Selection Process

The selection process may include a written exam followed by an interview or skill test, depending on the position applied for.

Admit Card

Candidates can download the admit card from the official website by entering their registration details.

Result

The result will be announced on the official website. Candidates can check their result by entering their roll number or registration details.
