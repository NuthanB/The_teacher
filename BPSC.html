BPSC-TRE (Bihar Public Service Commission Teacher Recruitment Examination) Guide

Introduction to BPSC-TRE Exam:

The Bihar Public Service Commission Teacher Recruitment Examination (BPSC-TRE) is conducted by the Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) to recruit teachers for various teaching positions in Bihar state government schools.

It is a competitive examination that assesses the eligibility and suitability of candidates for teaching roles.

Eligibility Criteria:

Eligibility criteria for BPSC-TRE exam:

  • Candidates must have a minimum educational qualification as specified by the BPSC.
  • There may be different eligibility criteria for primary, secondary, and higher secondary level teaching positions.
  • Candidates must fulfill age criteria as per government regulations.
  • Relaxation in age and educational qualifications may be provided to certain categories as per government norms.

Exam Pattern:

Level Subjects Number of Questions Total Marks Duration
Primary Level Child Development and Pedagogy, Language I, Language II, Mathematics, Environmental Studies 150 150 3 hours
Secondary Level Child Development and Pedagogy, Language I, Language II, Mathematics & Science, Social Studies/Social Science 150 150 3 hours

Syllabus:

The syllabus for BPSC-TRE exam includes:

  • Child Development and Pedagogy
  • Languages (Hindi, English, Sanskrit, Urdu)
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Studies (for Primary Level)
  • Science (for Secondary Level)
  • Social Studies/Social Science (for Secondary Level)

Preparation Tips:

  • Understand the exam pattern and syllabus thoroughly.
  • Practice previous years' question papers and mock tests.
  • Focus on strengthening subject-specific knowledge for the level you're applying for.
  • Refer to standard textbooks and study materials recommended by experts.
  • Stay updated with current affairs and educational trends.
  • Join coaching classes or online study groups for guidance and support.
  • Manage your time effectively during the exam and practice time-bound mock tests.
