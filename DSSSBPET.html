DSSSB Physical Education Teacher (PET) Exam Guide

Exam Pattern:

The DSSSB Physical Education Teacher (PET) Exam is conducted in multiple-choice question (MCQ) format.

The duration of the exam is usually 2 hours.

The paper consists of various sections covering different topics related to physical education.

Syllabus:

Section Syllabus
1. Anatomy and Physiology
  • Human Anatomy
  • Human Physiology
2. Sports Psychology
  • Understanding of Psychology in Sports
  • Motivation and Performance
3. Sports Management
  • Administration of Sports Programs
  • Facility Management
4. History and Philosophy of Physical Education
  • Evolution of Physical Education
  • Philosophical Foundations

Preparation Tips:

  • Understand the syllabus thoroughly and focus on important topics.
  • Refer to standard textbooks and study materials for each subject.
  • Practice previous years' question papers to understand the exam pattern.
  • Stay updated with recent developments in the field of physical education.
  • Engage in regular physical activities to understand practical aspects better.
