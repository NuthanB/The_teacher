DSSSB Nursery Teacher Exam Guide

Exam Pattern:

The DSSSB Nursery Teacher Exam consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

The duration of the exam is typically 2 hours.

The paper is divided into sections covering various subjects.

Syllabus:

Section Topics
Child Development and Pedagogy Theories of Child Development, Concepts of Learning and Pedagogical Issues, Understanding Children with Special Needs
Nursery Rhymes and Stories Famous Nursery Rhymes, Storytelling Techniques, Usage of Stories in Teaching
Mathematics Numbers and Number System, Basic Arithmetic Operations, Shapes and Spatial Understanding
English Language Skills Alphabets and Phonetics, Vocabulary Building, Basic Grammar
General Awareness Current Affairs, Social and Environmental Issues, Basic Knowledge of Indian History, Geography, and Culture

Preparation Tips:

  • Start by thoroughly understanding the syllabus and exam pattern.
  • Practice previous years' question papers to get acquainted with the exam format.
  • Focus on strengthening concepts in child development, nursery rhymes, basic mathematics, and language skills.
  • Stay updated with current affairs and general knowledge topics.
  • Develop storytelling skills and explore creative teaching methods.
  • Take mock tests to assess your preparation level and identify areas for improvement.
