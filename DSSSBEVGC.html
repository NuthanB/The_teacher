DSSSB EVGC Exam Guide

Exam Pattern:

The DSSSB EVGC Exam consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

The duration of the exam is typically 2 hours.

The paper is divided into sections covering various subjects.

Syllabus:

Subject Syllabus
Educational Psychology
  • Theories of Learning
  • Intelligence and Creativity
  • Personality Development
Counseling and Guidance
  • Counseling Theories and Techniques
  • Group Counseling
  • Career Counseling
Educational and Vocational Development
  • Education and Career Planning
  • Workplace Issues
  • Entrepreneurship

Preparation Tips:

  • Start by thoroughly understanding the syllabus and exam pattern.
  • Practice previous years' question papers to get acquainted with the exam format.
  • Focus on strengthening concepts in educational psychology, counseling techniques, and career development.
  • Stay updated with the latest trends in education and career guidance.
  • Develop communication and interpersonal skills necessary for counseling.
  • Take mock tests to assess your preparation level and identify areas for improvement.
