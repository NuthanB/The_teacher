About Ajay Singh Kharb




Ajay Singh Kharb is a Teacher Trainer & Mentor with 10+ years of experience. Every student aspires to study under him due to his unique Teaching style. He has cracked many government job exams such as DSSSB PRT, DSSSB EVGC Counsellor (PGT level), KVS PRT, CTET & many TET Exams. He did not pursue his job as he found that most of the students want to crack govt. job exams but are unable to do so due to lack of timely support, guidance, and quality education atmosphere. Therefore, he wanted to help & guide each student to turn their dreams into reality through his authentic and reliable support. Thousands of his students are employed at central as well as state level govt. jobs. (DSSSB Delhi, KVS, NVS, AEES, EMRS, NCERT, Chandigarh, Rajasthan, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, UGC NET (Assistant Professor) etc.) He has done extensive work on mental health and wellbeing and conducts seminars for Teachers of Delhi Government(SCERT). His counselling sessions have helped many individuals to lead life with confidence & adopt a positive approach towards it.

Why I Founded The Teacher?


During my time as an educator, I observed many talented students who wished to become teachers but were unable to due to a lack of proper guidance and access to complete information about the certification process. Without the right resources, many missed opportunities they were qualified for. Too often potential teachers fell through the cracks due to informational gaps. It was clear to me that with reliable advising, more aspiring teachers could fulfill their dreams.
This realization inspired me to create The Teacher, an online platform developed to address the void in guidance. On the site, any student considering the teaching profession can access authentic, current details on teacher exams, requirements, scholarships and more. By providing accessible, trustworthy information, The Teacher aims to level the playing field for all students and their dreams of teaching. With the right knowledge at the right time, the potential within these aspiring educators can be unlocked to enrich the lives of future students. The Teacher strives to empower students along their path to the classroom.
