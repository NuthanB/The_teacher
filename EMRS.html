EMRS Teaching Exam Information

Overview

The Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) conduct recruitment exams for various teaching positions. Below is detailed information about the exam:

Eligibility Criteria

Position Minimum Educational Qualification Age Limit
Principal Master's Degree with B.Ed. or equivalent and relevant experience Maximum 50 years
Vice Principal Master's Degree with B.Ed. or equivalent and relevant experience Maximum 45 years
Post Graduate Teacher (PGT) Master's Degree with B.Ed. or equivalent in the concerned subject Maximum 40 years
Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT) Bachelor's Degree with B.Ed. or equivalent in the concerned subject Maximum 35 years
Primary Teacher (PRT) Senior Secondary with D.El.Ed./B.El.Ed. or equivalent Maximum 30 years

Application Process

To apply for the EMRS teaching exam, candidates need to follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official website of EMRS.
  2. Register for the exam by providing necessary details.
  3. Fill in the online application form with accurate information.
  4. Upload scanned copies of required documents such as educational certificates, photograph, and signature.
  5. Pay the application fee online through the provided payment gateway.
  6. Submit the application form and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.

Syllabus

Section Topics
General English Grammar, vocabulary, comprehension, etc.
General Hindi व्याकरण, शब्दावली, पठन कौशल, etc.
General Knowledge & Current Affairs Indian history, geography, politics, current events, etc.
Numerical & Mathematical Ability Arithmetic, algebra, data interpretation, etc.
Reasoning Ability Logical reasoning, analytical reasoning, problem-solving, etc.
Teaching Aptitude Teaching methodology, pedagogical skills, educational psychology, etc.

Exam Pattern

The exam pattern for the EMRS teaching exam typically includes the following sections:

  • General English
  • General Hindi
  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs
  • Numerical & Mathematical Ability
  • Reasoning Ability
  • Teaching Aptitude

The exam may be conducted in online or offline mode and consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs). The number of questions and marks distribution vary based on the position and level of the exam.

Additional Details

Selection Process

The selection process may include a written exam followed by an interview or skill test, depending on the position applied for.

Admit Card

Candidates can download the admit card from the official website by entering their registration details.

Result

The result will be announced on the official website. Candidates can check their result by entering their roll number or registration details.
