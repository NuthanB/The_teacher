DSSSB TGT Exam Information

About the Exam

The DSSSB TGT exam is conducted by the Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) for the recruitment of Trained Graduate Teachers (TGTs) in various subjects for schools under the Government of National Capital Territory of Delhi (GNCTD).

Syllabus

The syllabus for the DSSSB TGT exam includes the following subjects:

  • General Awareness
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability
  • Numerical Ability & Data Interpretation
  • Arithmetical & Numerical Ability
  • English Language & Comprehension
  • Hindi Language & Comprehension
  • Subject-specific topics based on the chosen subject (e.g., Mathematics, Science, Social Science, etc.)

Exam Pattern

The exam pattern for the DSSSB TGT exam is as follows:

Section Number of Questions Marks per Question Total Marks
General Awareness 20 1 20
General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability 20 1 20
Numerical Ability & Data Interpretation 20 1 20
Arithmetical & Numerical Ability 20 1 20
English Language & Comprehension 20 1 20
Hindi Language & Comprehension 20 1 20
Subject-specific 100 1 100

Exam Preparation Tips

  • Understand the exam pattern and syllabus thoroughly.
  • Make a study schedule and stick to it.
  • Practice previous years' question papers and mock tests.
  • Focus on strengthening your subject knowledge.
  • Improve time management skills to solve questions within the given time.
  • Stay updated with current affairs and relevant topics.
