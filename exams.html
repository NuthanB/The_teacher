CTET ▼

The Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET) is an entrance exam conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) in India. It's mandatory for individuals aspiring to become teachers in government schools (from Class 1 to Class 8).

Exam Name CTET
Exam Details Paper 1 is for candidates who intend to teach classes I to V (Primary stage).
Syllabus Child Development and Pedagogy , Language I (usually the medium of instruction), Language II (chosen from a list other than Language I), Mathematics, Environmental Studies
Total Questions 150 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs), 30 questions from each section.
Exam Duration 2 hours and 30 minutes.
