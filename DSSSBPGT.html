DSSSB PGT Exam Information

About the Exam

The DSSSB PGT exam is conducted by the Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) to recruit Post Graduate Teachers for various subjects.

Syllabus

The syllabus for the DSSSB PGT exam varies based on the subject chosen. However, it generally includes topics from the respective subject's curriculum at the postgraduate level.

Exam Pattern

Section Number of Questions Marks
General Awareness 20 20
General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability 20 20
Arithmetical & Numerical Ability 20 20
Test of Hindi Language & Comprehension 20 20
Test of English Language & Comprehension 20 20
Subject Concerned (MCQs) 100 100

Exam Tips

  • Understand the syllabus thoroughly and focus on key topics.
  • Practice previous years' question papers to understand the exam pattern.
  • Improve time management skills by solving mock tests within the allotted time.
  • Stay updated with current affairs and developments related to the subject.
  • Revise regularly and maintain a healthy study routine.
